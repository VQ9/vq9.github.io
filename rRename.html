rRename


rRename is a plugin that allows you to rename items. It also allows you to use hexadecimal color codes (e. g. #7a0000). It's highly customizable. Example config:
version: 1.0.0 appearance: prefix: rRename prefix-color: 771177 color-normal: FB336D color-error: 7a0000 economy: #When enabled, rRename removes a specific amount of money from the players balance when renaming an item. enabled: false price: 50
